1. Product Vision (What You’re Really Selling)
You are NOT selling a ROPA document.
You are selling:
“A continuous data processing record management solution that keeps organizations compliant at all times.”
So the positioning becomes:
•	Not one-off
•	Not static
•	Always up-to-date
•	Audit-ready
________________________________________
🚀 2. Product Model: Subscription Structure
Free Trial
•	7 days free trial
•	No payment required upfront (or optional card capture)
•	Limited features (important)
________________________________________
Paid Tiers (Recommended Structure)
🟢 Tier 1: Starter (Basic Compliance)
Best for: Small businesses / startups
What’s included:
•	ROPA template (standardized)
•	Guided onboarding (questionnaire-based)
•	Up to 3 processing activities
•	Manual updates
•	Downloadable ROPA (Excel/PDF)
•	Email support
Limitations:
•	No automation
•	No multi-user access
•	Limited scalability
________________________________________
🔵 Tier 2: Growth (Operational Compliance)
Best for: Growing companies / SMEs
What’s included:
•	Everything in Starter PLUS:
•	Up to 15 processing activities
•	Dashboard view of data processing
•	Editable and dynamic ROPA
•	Version history tracking
•	Basic risk flagging (e.g., sensitive data alerts)
•	Multi-department input (limited users)
•	Data export formats
•	Priority support
________________________________________
🟣 Tier 3: Enterprise (Advanced Compliance & Governance)
Best for: Large organizations / multi-jurisdiction
What’s included:
•	Everything in Growth PLUS:
•	Unlimited processing activities
•	Full automation workflows
•	Role-based access control
•	Integration with DPIA module (future upsell)
•	Third-party processor tracking
•	Audit logs & compliance reporting
•	Multi-jurisdiction compliance mapping (NDPA, GDPR, etc.)
•	Dedicated account support
________________________________________
💰 3. Pricing Strategy (Global Positioning)
Since you want to position globally, avoid naira pricing.
Use USD pricing (with local equivalents where needed).
Suggested Pricing:
•	Starter: $15 – $29 / month
•	Growth: $49 – $99 / month
•	Enterprise: $150 – $300+ / month (customizable)
👉 OR annual pricing:
•	Offer 20% discount for yearly plans

🧠 5. Feature Strategy (Very Important)
You need to intentionally hold back features per tier.
Feature	Starter	Growth	Enterprise
ROPA Template	✅	✅	✅
Processing Activities	3	15	Unlimited
Dashboard	❌	✅	✅
Automation	❌	❌	✅
Multi-user	❌	Limited	Full
Compliance Mapping	❌	Basic	Advanced
Audit Logs	❌	❌	✅
👉 This is what drives upgrades.
